How the Model Works

This flight price prediction system is powered by a machine learning model built using the Random Forest Regressor algorithm. To enhance the model's performance, hyperparameter tuning was performed using the RandomizedSearchCV technique. The model was evaluated using standard regression metrics. On the training data, it achieved an RMSE of 0.0672, MSE of 0.0045, and MAE of 0.0280. Meanwhile, on the validation data, it reached an RMSE of 0.1097, MSE of 0.0120, and MAE of 0.0468, showing strong generalization ability.

How to Predict Flight Price

To get a flight price prediction, simply click the Start Now button on the homepage. You will be redirected to a form page where you can input details such as the airline, departure time, source and destination cities, and more. Once the form is filled in, click the Predict button, and the system will instantly show the estimated ticket price based on your inputs.
